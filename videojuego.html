"Interactive Educational Game with Chatbot: An educational game focused on the exploration of exoplanets."

This game focuses on learning about exoplanets, but it has a unique feature that distinguishes it from other educational games. It includes a narrative voice designed to make the experience more accessible and comfortable for visually impaired children.

. Additionally, the game offers the option to disable the narration for those who prefer to play without it.

The game centers around a group of astronauts whose ship has suffered damage. To repair it and return home, they must explore various exoplanets, delving into their depths in search of essential materials.

Each planet is filled with adventures and challenges, and players will receive help from a chatbot that provides hints and guidance while they learn along the way.

This project is the result of our initial forays into programming and art, carried out with limited time and resources.

However, we are excited about the future, where we hope to develop even more innovative ideas and projects that can contribute to solving problems in society.
